My Projects


Watch to Edit : Video Retargeting using gaze

With the advent of large number of viewing screens in the day-to-day lives of people, there also rises a need to retarget movies/theater sequences which are usually shot with a specific aspect ratio (usually 16:9). This project provides a novel approach to pragmatically solve the problem to retargeting. [Paper Accepted at Eurographics 2018 yay!!]

Automated Split-Screen Composition

The main objective behind Split Screen Automation is to enhance the viewing experience allowing to better understand and apprehend the emotions depicted by the actors and their reactions/responses. Split Screen Composition involves displaying different video streams simultaneously and dividing the viewing space into multiple parts. The video is shot with one high resolution camera (Upper Half) and the requiredSplit Screen composition (lower Half) are generated using tracks such that they follow common videograpy practices. [Paper Accepted at Eurographics 2017 yay!]

Sports video Analysis

The project is an interesting application of a combination of image processing and semantic soccer logic in order to generate information about a broadcast soccer video. The aim of the project was to detect and track ball , players and to detect events such as goals , passes etc.[PDF]

Age-Gender-Ethinicity Estimation

This project aimed at making a model that would given a picture of a person guess the age, gender and ethincity of the person.The project involved detecting the face of person and extracting features and classifying them into predefined classes.

Flood Shelter Locator

The project's novel idea was to locate flood shelters for a given area considering various factors such as Population Density, Distance from the coast, Altitude map of the area, conectivity of the area and most importantly imnundation maps of the area.
